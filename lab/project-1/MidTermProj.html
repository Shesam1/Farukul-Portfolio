To manufacture and supply the finest quality textile products on time. — Consumer Mission

We believe our first responsibility is to our buyers and end consumers who use our products. It is paramount that we ensure the highest quality to meet their needs. As a production-oriented business, we also need to maintain competitive prices through cost reduction. Clients should always receive their desired order on time and we will always continue to maintain relationship with them.

Our History

In 1980 , Md. Nurul Islam who is founder of Noman Group, started his journey with a vision of establishing NG as the largest textile manufacturer in the world. With sustained growth over the years and an ever-expanding list of international clientele, it has solidified its position as a leading vertically integrated textile group in Bangladesh
